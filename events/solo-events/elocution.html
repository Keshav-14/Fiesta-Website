Elocution

Faculty In-charge : Dr. J. Deepalakshmi/MATH

Rules

  1. Reporting Time : 1:15 PM

    2. Topic:

  2. 1. விடாமுயற்சி மற்றும் உறுதியுடன் சவால்களை சமாளிக்க ஒவ்வொரு குழந்தைக்கும் வாசிப்பு ஏன் அவசியம்.
  3. 2. என்னை செதுக்கிய இரண்டு இலக்கிய புத்தகங்கள்.
  4. Participants should speak on any one the above topics only in Pure Tamil- தூய தமிழ்
  5. The speech will be for 5 minutes. A reminder will be given at 4th minute.
  6. Voice and tone modulations of the participants will be considered.
  7. Participants should have their content and may show their creative touch during the event
  8. Vulgarity and provocative thoughts in the topic or talk will lead to disqualification from the event
  9. Participants will not be allowed to take any paper or material with them during the speech.
  10. Judging criteria will be: Clarity of voice, Fluency, Content and Creativity, Posture plus gesture
  11. The Jury’s decision is final.
