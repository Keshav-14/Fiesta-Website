Divide and Conquer

Faculty In-charge : Mr.Sebastiyan/LIB

Rules

  1. Reporting Time : 01:15 PM
  2. The team can consist of 5 members; Only 20 teams can register.
  3. Registration based on first come first serve basis.
  4. No change of team members is permitted once registration is closed.
  5. There are totally three rounds to finish the game i.e. Prelims, Round 1 and the Final round
  6. Prelims would be conducted on the same day of the event Maximum of 10 teams will be selected from prelims.
  7. Time Duration: 2 Hrs (20 min-Prelims, 45 min-Round 1, 45 min-Final Round)
  8. Out of 20 teams, 10 Teams will be selected to Round 1 and 5 teams to the final round.
  9. Multiple tasks will be given to the participants in each team separately at each level.
  10. Marks will be provided on the basis of time of completion of the tasks collectively
  11. The Jury's decision is final.
