Treasure Hunt

Faculty In-charge : Dr.J.Senthil Kumar/ECE

Rules

  1. The participating team must have exactly 5 members. There will be no changes in the participants list after initial submission.
  2. A total of 30 teams (15 Boys teams and 15 Girls teams) will be shortlisted for prelims round based on first come first served.
  3. Participants should report to venue before 9:10am. Prelims round starts by 9:15am.
  4. Elimination will be there based on your prelims score.
  5. 15 teams will be shortlisted for the item collection round.
  6. If you borrow any items within the campus from anybody you must be responsible for returning it.
  7. Interaction among other teams is strictly prohibited.
  8. For the final round, 8 teams will be shortlisted.
  9. Participants must bring their own materials
  10. Final score will be calculated based on the performance from previous levels.
  11. Be genuine and ethical to solve the clues at each level on your own. Googling the answers is strictly prohibited.
  12. The Jury's decision is final.
