Rangoli

Faculty In-charge : Mrs. Blessa Binolin Pepsi/IT & Mrs.P.Madhumitha/ENG

Rules

  1. Maximum of 3 participants per team.
  2. Theme : NATIONAL INTEGRITY
  3. Time Duration: 90 min; Reporting Time: 10:00 am.
  4. Can use rock salt, flowers, dhal etc. & Should bring the necessary items
  5. Usage of templates are strictly prohibited.
  6. Judging criteria will be: symmetry, colour combination, innovation, creativity and the neatness of the Rangoli.
  7. The Jury's decision is final.
