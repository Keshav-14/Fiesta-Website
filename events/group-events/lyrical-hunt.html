Lyrical Hunt

Faculty In-charge : Mrs.S.Usha/CIV

Rules

  1. Reporting Time : 01:15 PM
  2. Each team should have 3 members
  3. The Event comprises of 2 segments - Prelims followed by the Main Event.
  4. Prelims will be conducted and 8 teams will be selected for the main round
  5. Prelims - 3 segments; each segment - 10 questions & each question - 1 point
  6. Questions for prelims - in the form of jumbled lyric, complete the lyric and guess the song
  7. Main round will consist of 5 to 6 segments like Karaoke, Connections, Rapid Fire, Reverse the song, etc.,
  8. Marks for each segment will be announced on the spot.
  9. •The Jury’s decision is final.
