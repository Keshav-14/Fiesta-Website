Radio-mirchi

Faculty In-charge : Mrs.P.Palniladevi, Asst. Professor/ECE

Rules

  1. Reporting Time : 10:15 AM
  2. A team can have a maximum of three members. Single member per team is acceptable.
  3. Recorded voices or sounds are not allowed.
  4. Voice and tone modulations of the participants will be considered.
  5. Participants should have their content relevant to their titles and may show their creative touch during the show.
  6. The event as a whole has two rounds.
    1. 1st round- participants can prepare their talk with their own topics.
    2. 2nd round- participants will be given a task to find the topic and are expected to talk on the topic.
  7. Vulgarity and provocative thoughts in the topic or talk will lead to disqualification from the event.
  8. JUDGEMENT CRITERIA:
    1. Clarity of voice 2. Fluency 3. Originality 4. Communication of the contest theme 5. Sense of humour and presentation.
  9. The Jury's decision is final.
